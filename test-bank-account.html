🇨🇱 Chilean Bank Account Setup - Test Guide

✅ System Ready!

The country-first bank account system has been successfully implemented. Here's how Chilean users can now add their accounts:

Step 1: Select Chile 🌍

Available countries: Chile, United States, Germany, Spain, France, Italy, Mexico, United Kingdom

Step 2: Choose Your Bank 🏦

Chilean banks available:

  • Banco de Chile (Code: 001)
  • Banco del Estado (BancoEstado) (Code: 012)
  • Banco de Crédito e Inversiones (BCI) (Code: 016)
  • Banco Santander Chile (Code: 037)
  • Banco Itaú Chile (Code: 039)
  • Banco Security (Code: 049)
  • Banco Falabella (Code: 051)
  • And 4 more major banks...

Step 3: Select Account Type 💳

Chilean account types:

  • Cuenta Corriente (Checking Account)
  • Cuenta de Ahorro (Savings Account)
  • Cuenta Vista (Sight Account)
  • Cuenta RUT (BancoEstado only)

Step 4: Enter Account Details 📝

RUT Format Examples:
✅ 12.345.678-9
✅ 8.765.432-1
✅ 15.876.543-K

Note: RUT validation and auto-formatting included!

🎉 What's Improved

  • No Manual Bank Entry: Choose from predefined banks instead of typing
  • Country-First Approach: Makes sense for multinational currencies like EUR
  • Simplified Chilean Flow: Only RUT + Account Number needed (bank code auto-filled)
  • Smart Validation: RUT format validation with automatic formatting
  • Better UX: Clear 3-step process with visual indicators
  • Multi-Country Support: Works with 8 countries and their banking systems

🚀 Ready to Test

To test the new Chilean bank account system:

  1. Go to Dashboard
  2. Click "Send to User" or "Send to Bank"
  3. If you don't have a verified account, you'll see the new onboarding flow
  4. Select "Chile" from the country dropdown
  5. Choose any Chilean bank
  6. Fill in RUT and account number
  7. Submit and verify!
